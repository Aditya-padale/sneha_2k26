About the Fest

Powering a celebration
like no other

Sneha 2k26 is not just a cultural fest — it's the heartbeat of our campus. A two-day spectacle where talent meets passion and tradition meets modernity.

Live Music15+ Artists

World-class Performances

From classical Bharatanatyam to hip-hop fusion — witness talent that pushes creative boundaries across 40+ events and two electrifying days.

Community at the Heart

Student organizers, faculty guides, and 100+ passionate volunteers come together to deliver an unforgettable shared experience for thousands.

Competitions & Awards

Compete across 20+ disciplines with a ₹5 Lakh prize pool and certificates recognized across universities nationwide.

ExhibitionArt & Fashion

Art & Fashion Exhibition

An immersive gallery of student artwork, live installations, and a runway showcase celebrating individual expression and creative identity.

Food & Culture Bazaar

A vibrant marketplace of flavors and crafts from across India — street food, handmade goods, and cultural stalls that bring the nation to one campus.
